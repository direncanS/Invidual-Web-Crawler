\# T03 Email Setup (MailHog)



\## Goal

Send reset password emails to MailHog in local environment.



\## Requirements

\- SMTP host: mailhog

\- SMTP port: 1025

\- Email appears in MailHog UI on http://localhost:8025

\- Reset link or token is visible in the captured email



\## Evidence

\- Screenshot of MailHog email

\- Reset works within 60 seconds
